# CMO Strategy Report: Project CurveStream

## 1. Target Persona
**Persona:** "Chloe, 29, Body-Positive Content Creator & Influencer"
*   **Background:** Chloe has 150k followers on TikTok and Instagram. She is currently frustrated with OnlyFans because her content is buried in an algorithm that favors "traditional" beauty standards, and she feels her community is undervalued by the platform’s generic discovery tools.
*   **Pain Points:** High platform fees (20%), lack of niche-specific discoverability, constant "shadowbanning" for using keywords like "plus-size," and feeling like a "second-class citizen" on mainstream adult platforms.
*   **Motivations:** She wants a platform that celebrates her body type as the *default* rather than an *exception*, better tools to monetize her specific fan base, and a community where she doesn't have to fight for visibility.

---

## 2. 3-Channel Acquisition Strategy

### Channel 1: "The Migration" (Influencer Affiliate Program)
*   **Tactic:** Instead of broad ads, we recruit 50 "Founding Creators" (like Chloe). We offer them a **90/10 revenue share** for the first 6 months if they move their primary link-in-bio to CurveStream.
*   **Execution:** Direct outreach via Instagram DMs and Email. We provide them with a "CurveStream Migration Kit," which includes pre-made social media assets that highlight the platform’s body-positive mission, allowing them to frame the move as "joining a movement" rather than just a platform change.

### Channel 2: Programmatic SEO & Content Hub
*   **Tactic:** Build a "Body Positivity Resource Hub" on the site (outside the paywall). This hub will host articles and SEO-optimized landing pages targeting long-tail keywords such as "best plus-size adult creators," "curvy community platforms," and "body positive adult content."
*   **Execution:** We use AI to generate blog content around body-positivity culture, which drives organic search traffic. These pages act as high-conversion funnels that drive users to creator profile previews.

### Channel 3: Targeted "Niche-Specific" Reddit & Forum Takeover
*   **Tactic:** Establish an official presence in hyper-targeted subreddits (e.g., r/PlusSize, r/BBW, r/CurvyModels). 
*   **Execution:** We do not spam. We sponsor "Creator Spotlight" threads where we pay popular niche creators to host Q&As on our platform. By sponsoring these communities, we gain the trust of the core demographic and drive high-intent traffic directly to our sign-up page.
