### 3. Runway & Break-Even Analysis

**Funding Needed for 12-Month Runway:**
To cover 12 months of operations with a 20% contingency buffer for legal/regulatory emergencies:
*   ($36,350 x 12) * 1.20 = **$523,440**

**Break-Even Calculation:**
*   **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** We estimate a weighted average of $22.00/month across our three tiers.
*   **Net Margin per User:** After payment processing (10%), hosting/platform costs (15%), and creator revenue share (15%), our net margin is **60%**.
*   **Net Contribution per User:** $22.00 * 0.60 = **$13.20**.
*   **Monthly Break-Even Goal:** $36,350 / $13.20 = **2,754 Active Subscribers.**
